Introduce

14 Jan 2021

Currently, the parking lot is increasingly crowded, leading to difficulties in choosing quality parking, or when the parking space is out of space, the car is stolen,â€¦. This is the milestone SCPS website was born. SCPS means: "Smart Car Parking System".



Technology


* IOT : ESP8266 NodeMCU, Ultrasonic Sensor, DC Servo Motor, IR Sensors, 16x2 i2c LCD Display, Jumpers

* Database : Firebase

* Web Host: GitHub

* Framework: Angular



Detail

Step1: When the vehicle enters the parking lot, the sensor will detect it and check the sensor will scan it to detect the vehicle and enable Servo for the vehicle to enter.

Step 2: Next, the car will park in the area of the parking lot. There will be 1-2 parking spaces corresponding to area numbers A, B, C

Step 3: When the vehicle is parked in the above area, the IR Sensor will scan the vehicle and upload the information to Firebase and display it on the Website.

Step 4: When the car leaves the parking lot, the time is saved and the parking data will return to null.
